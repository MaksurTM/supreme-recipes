Protein Oat Cake

: breakfast calories


Macros

  • Calories: 464
  • Protein: 44g
  • Carbs: 56g
  • Fat: 7g

Ingredients

  • 60g oats
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 100ml of almond milk or milk of choice
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup of whey protein

Instructions

  1. Blend the oats to make flour.
  2. Add the egg, baking powder, vanilla extract, milk and banana.
  3. Blend.
  4. Add one cup of whey protein.
  5. Blend again.
  6. Add to a greased baking dish and microwave for 2.5 to 3 minutes.
  7. Optionally, add whatever toppings you like.
